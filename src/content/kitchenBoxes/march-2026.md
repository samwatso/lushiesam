March brings a five-product spring box with strong nostalgia value. Sakura is the headline — soft pink bathwater, embedded flower petals, and a delicate cherry blossom scent that echoes the 2018 original. The Honey Bee soap channels the beloved HIWTK scent family, and Avobath adds vivid emerald green bathwater with an energising lemongrass kick. Twilight Body Lotion and Lime Pastille Shower Jelly complete a well-rounded, variety-heavy month.
