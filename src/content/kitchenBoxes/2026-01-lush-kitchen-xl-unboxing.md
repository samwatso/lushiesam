## Eye mask

![Eye mask](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_0_eye_mask.jpg)

**Scent family:** Sleepy
> A soothing eye mask infused with the calming, dreamy scent of the beloved Sleepy range — lavender and tonka bean — designed to help you drift off into a restful slumber.
**Nostalgia:** Part of Lush's beloved Sleepy range, a fan-favourite sleep-aid collection that has built a devoted following since its launch
**Archive:** The Sleepy eye mask is a Kitchen and exclusive product designed as part of the broader Sleepy sleep range
**Throwback:** Companion product to the iconic Sleepy body lotion and Twilight bath bomb, extending the lavender-tonka sleep ritual

## Butterball

![Butterball](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_1_butterball.jpg)

**Scent family:** Vanilla
> BODY LOTION: Get soft and snuggly with this dreamy cream of moisturising cocoa butter and almond oil, with a comforting vanilla — like fragrance.
**Nostalgia:** Butterball is one of Lush's most enduring and beloved bath bombs, a staple since the early days and a perennial fan favourite for its simple, skin-loving luxury
**Archive:** One of Lush's longest-running products, Butterball has been a mainstay on the main line for decades before appearing as Kitchen iterations
**Throwback:** The original Butterball bath bomb, a Lush classic known for its cocoa butter and almond oil skin-softening properties

## Sleepy

![Sleepy](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_2_sleepy.jpg)

**Scent family:** Sleepy
> SHOWER GUMMY: When twilight settles in, wash the day away with sweet and comforting malty lavender suds.
**Nostalgia:** The Sleepy range took the Lush world by storm and became a cult phenomenon, with fans swearing by its sleep-inducing lavender and tonka scent — this shower gummy brings that magic into the bathroom routine
**Archive:** The Sleepy scent family launched as a body lotion and expanded into a full range of products; shower gummy formats are a newer Kitchen innovation
**Throwback:** A shower gummy interpretation of the iconic Sleepy body lotion, carrying the same malty lavender and tonka bean scent

## Forty Winks

![Forty Winks](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_3_forty_winks.jpg)

**Scent family:** Lavender
> MASSAGE BAR: Lavender and rosemary clear the fog while tarragon banishes apathy, so after a good massage you should feel just about ready to tackle anything! LAVENDER VIDA LOCA Soap Flourishes of lavender and sweet chamomile help soothe your skin and clear your mind so you can sink into a peaceful slumber.
**Nostalgia:** Forty Winks has long been a cherished product in Lush's sleep and relaxation repertoire, beloved by fans who use it as part of a bedtime massage ritual
**Archive:** Forty Winks massage bar has appeared in various formats across Lush's main line and Kitchen over the years, consistently featuring lavender and rosemary as hero notes
**Throwback:** Part of Lush's sleep-focused product family alongside Sleepy and Dream Time, sharing the lavender-led relaxation theme

## Lavender Vida Loca

![Lavender Vida Loca](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_4_lavender_vida_loca.jpg)

**Scent family:** Lavender
> Soap: flourishes of lavender and sweet chamomile help sooth your skin and clear your mind so you can sink into a peaceful slumber.
**Nostalgia:** A playful nod to Lush's Vida Loca naming tradition, beloved by fans who enjoy Lush's whimsical product names paired with genuinely calming formulas
**Archive:** Lavender Vida Loca is a Kitchen-exclusive soap that fits into the January 2026 sleep-themed box, featuring lavender and chamomile as key botanicals
**Throwback:** Shares its calming lavender and chamomile spirit with the broader Lush sleep range, acting as a cleansing companion to products like Sleepy and Forty Winks

## Softy

![Softy](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_5_softy.jpg)

**Scent family:** Marshmallow
> FOOT LOTION: Kick back and relax as a dreamy blend of rich butters and marshmallow root soften your soles.
**Nostalgia:** Softy foot lotion is a Kitchen gem that fans of Lush's indulgent body care line adore for its gentle, comforting formula perfect for tired feet
**Archive:** Softy has appeared as a Kitchen exclusive foot lotion, celebrated for its marshmallow root and rich butter blend aimed at intense foot softening
**Throwback:** Evokes the same cosy, marshmallow-soft comfort as products like Sleepy, making it a natural bedtime foot-care companion

## Dream Time

![Dream Time](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_6_dream_time.jpg)

**Scent family:** Sleepy
> NAKED BATH OIL: A small but mighty brick of luxurious cocoa butter to moisturise your skin and all the most calming oils we could think of.
**Nostalgia:** Naked bath oils are a fan-favourite Kitchen format, and Dream Time resonates deeply with those who love Lush's sleep-focused range and the ritual of a calming evening bath
**Archive:** Dream Time naked bath oil is a Kitchen exclusive that fits within Lush's growing sleep and relaxation product family, using a naked/solid format to reduce packaging
**Throwback:** A solid bath oil companion to the Sleepy and Twilight range, channelling the same calming, sleep-inducing botanical oils in a luxurious cocoa butter base

## Golden slumbers

![Golden slumbers](/images/boxes/2026-01-lush-kitchen-xl-unboxing/product_7_golden_slumbers.jpg)

**Scent family:** Lavender
> BATH BOMB: Soak in glittering golden waters of calming chamomile and lavender, while a clearing shot of neroli cuts through your thoughts.
**Nostalgia:** The name is a nod to the beloved Beatles song, instantly evoking warmth and nostalgia — fans of Lush's sleep range adore this bath bomb for combining glittering, golden visuals with genuinely calming botanicals
**Archive:** Golden Slumbers bath bomb has appeared as a Kitchen and seasonal exclusive, celebrated for its golden glittering waters and sleep-focused botanical blend of chamomile, lavender, and neroli
**Throwback:** Shares its lavender and chamomile sleep-inducing DNA with the wider Lush sleep range, with neroli adding a brightening, clarifying twist reminiscent of products like Twilight
